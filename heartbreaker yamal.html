About the Heartbreaker Boots

The Adidas F50 "Football's Heartbreaker" boots are the new signature boots worn by Barcelona star Lamine Yamal. They feature a white base color with red rose graphics and black Adidas stripes.

The rose design was inspired by Barcelona's Sant Jordi festival which celebrates roses and love. The idea behind the boots is that Yamal breaks defenders' hearts with his skillful dribbling and creative attacking play.

Performance Technology

  • Sprintframe 360 soleplate for acceleration
  • Laceless design for a clean striking surface
